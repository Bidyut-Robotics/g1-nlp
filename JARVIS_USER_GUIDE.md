# Jarvis — User Interaction Guide

Jarvis is a humanoid robot assistant deployed at Jindal Steel, Delhi.
This guide explains how to start a conversation, what you can ask, and how to end it.

---

## Starting a Conversation

**Say the wake word:**

> **"Hey Jarvis"**

Jarvis will respond: *"Yes? How can I help you?"*

You are now in conversation mode. You do not need to say "Hey Jarvis" again for follow-up questions — Jarvis listens continuously for 30 seconds after each response.

**Tip:** You can say your question in the same breath as the wake word:
> "Hey Jarvis, what time is it?"

Jarvis will skip the acknowledgement and answer directly.

---

## What You Can Ask

### Time & Date
| Say | Jarvis responds |
|---|---|
| "What time is it?" | Current time |
| "What is today's date?" | Today's date |
| "What day is it?" | Day of the week |

### About Jarvis
| Say | Jarvis responds |
|---|---|
| "Who are you?" | Name and role |
| "What can you do?" | List of capabilities |
| "Where are we?" | Current location |
| "Who do you work for?" | Jindal Steel |

### General Questions
Jarvis can answer general knowledge questions in English.

> "What is the capital of France?"
> "How many planets are in the solar system?"
> "What is the speed of light?"

**Note:** Jarvis answers only from what it knows. If it does not have specific information — such as employee names, internal company data, or confidential details — it will say so.

---

## Physical Interactions

### Handshake
> "Shake my hand" / "Can you shake my hand?" / "Give me a handshake"

Jarvis will extend its hand. Please extend yours to complete the handshake.

### Wave
Jarvis waves automatically when greeting or saying goodbye.

### Movement
| Say | Action |
|---|---|
| "Come forward" / "Move forward" | Jarvis takes a step toward you |
| "Go back" / "Move backward" | Jarvis steps back |

---

## Interrupting Jarvis (Barge-in)

You can interrupt Jarvis mid-sentence at any time by simply speaking.

- Jarvis will **stop speaking immediately**
- Your question will be **captured and answered**
- No need to say the wake word again

> **Example:** Jarvis is explaining something. You say *"Wait, what is the time?"* — Jarvis stops and answers your question.

---

## Ending the Conversation

| Say | What happens |
|---|---|
| "Goodbye" / "Bye" | Jarvis waves and returns to standby |
| "Stop" / "Never mind" | Jarvis stops and returns to standby |
| "Thank you, that's all" | Jarvis says goodbye |

Jarvis also returns to standby automatically after **30 seconds of silence**.

---

## Conversation Flow — Full Example

```
You:    "Hey Jarvis"
Jarvis: "Yes? How can I help you?"

You:    "What time is it?"
Jarvis: "It is 10:30 AM."

You:    "What is photosynthesis?"
Jarvis: "Photosynthesis is the process by which plants use sunlight,
         water, and carbon dioxide to produce oxygen and energy..."

You:    "Wait — where are we right now?"   ← interrupts mid-sentence
Jarvis: [stops speaking] "We are at Jindal Steel, Delhi."

You:    "Can you shake my hand?"
Jarvis: "Of course! Please extend your hand."
        [extends hand, holds 2 seconds, retracts]

You:    "Goodbye"
Jarvis: "Alright, goodbye! I'll be here if you need anything."
        [waves hand]
```

---

## Tips for Best Results

- **Speak clearly** in English, facing the robot
- **Normal speaking volume** — no need to shout
- **One question at a time** works best
- If Jarvis misheard you, just say "Hey Jarvis" again to restart
- Jarvis does not know confidential company information, employee names, or internal documents

---

## What Jarvis Cannot Do

- Access the internet in real time
- Look up specific employee or executive names
- Access internal company systems or documents
- Understand languages other than English
- Answer questions outside its knowledge
